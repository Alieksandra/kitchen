Frozen Desserts — Solo Scoops

Simple Vanilla
Ice Cream

Low sugar · No egg · Cuisinart Solo Scoops

10 min
2 hrs+
~20 min
~275g

Ingredients

50gWhole milk
35gGranulated sugar
175gValio whipping cream
½ tspVanilla powder
1 pinchFine salt
2 tabletsHermesetas (optional)

Method

  1. Whisk together milk, sugar, vanilla powder, salt and crushed Hermesetas tablets (if using) until fully dissolved.

  2. Stir in the whipping cream. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

  3. Turn the Solo Scoops ON first, then pour the chilled base through the funnel. Churn ~20 minutes until thickened.

  4. Transfer to an airtight container. Freeze 2 hours for a firmer scoop. Rest 10–15 min before serving.

Tips — Always start the machine before pouring. Never store ice cream in the freezer bowl. Sugar is reduced from the original 60g to 35g; the higher cream ratio compensates for texture. Add Hermesetas tablets to taste if you'd like more sweetness without affecting the freeze.
