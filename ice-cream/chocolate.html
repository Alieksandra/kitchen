Frozen Desserts — Solo Scoops

Simple Chocolate
Ice Cream

Low sugar · No egg · Cuisinart Solo Scoops

10 min
2 hrs+
~20 min
~290g

Ingredients

30gCocoa powder, sifted
35gBrown sugar
2 tabletsHermesetas (optional)
1 pinchFine salt
75gWhole milk
150gValio lactose-free whipping cream
¼ tspVanilla powder

Method

  1. Whisk together cocoa powder, brown sugar, salt and crushed Hermesetas tablets (if using) in a bowl.

  2. Add milk and whisk until sugar is fully dissolved.

  3. Stir in the cream and vanilla powder until combined. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

  4. Turn the Solo Scoops ON first, then pour the chilled base through the funnel. Churn ~20 minutes until thickened.

  5. Transfer to an airtight container. Freeze 2 hours for a firmer scoop. Rest 10–15 min before serving.

Tips — Brown sugar only — its molasses notes complement cocoa beautifully without the need for white sugar. Use high quality dark cocoa for a richer flavour. Never store ice cream in the freezer bowl. Add extra Hermesetas if you prefer it sweeter.
