Frozen Desserts — Solo Scoops

Strawberry
Cheesecake Ice Cream

Low sugar · Cream cheese base · Cuisinart Solo Scoops

15 min
Overnight
~20 min
~400g

Ingredients

115gCream cheese, room temp
38gGranulated sugar
1 pinchFine salt
2 tabletsHermesetas (optional)
75gWhole milk
¼ tspVanilla powder
1 tbspPlain yogurt, room temp
50gSliced strawberries
50gDigestive biscuits, chopped

Method

  1. Beat cream cheese, sugar, salt and crushed Hermesetas tablets (if using) with a hand mixer until smooth and creamy.

  2. Gradually add milk and vanilla powder, mixing until fully combined. Stir in the yogurt.

  3. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

  4. Turn the Solo Scoops ON first, then pour the chilled base through the funnel. Churn ~15 minutes.

  5. Add strawberries and digestive biscuit pieces through the funnel in the last 5 minutes of churning.

  6. Transfer to an airtight container. Freeze 2 hours for a firmer scoop. Rest 15 min before serving.

Tips — Sugar reduced from 65g to 38g — the cream cheese fat and natural strawberry sweetness compensate well. Strain the base before churning if it looks lumpy. Never store ice cream in the freezer bowl. Add Hermesetas tablets if you'd like more sweetness without affecting texture.
