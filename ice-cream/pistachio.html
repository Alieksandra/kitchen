Frozen Desserts — Solo Scoops

Pistachio
Ice Cream

Custard base · Cuisinart Solo Scoops · ~290ml batch

30 min
Overnight
~20 min
~290g

Ingredients

63gRaw pistachios (shelled)
50gPistachio paste (from above)
100gValio whipping cream
100gWhole milk
23gBrown sugar
1Egg yolk (large)
¼ tspVanilla powder
1 pinchFine sea salt

Method

  1. Toast pistachios at 160°C for 4–5 min (pre-roasted) or 9–10 min (raw). Cool slightly.

  2. Blend nuts on high, scraping down every few minutes, until a smooth runny paste forms — 10–15 min. Set aside to cool.

  3. Whisk cream, milk, sugar, egg yolk and salt in a small saucepan. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until it reaches 72°C.

  4. Sieve into a bowl. Whisk in 50g pistachio paste and vanilla powder. Press cling film onto the surface and refrigerate overnight.

  5. Turn the Solo Scoops ON first, then pour the chilled custard through the funnel. Churn ~20 minutes.

  6. Transfer to an airtight container. Freeze 1–2 hours for a firmer scoop. Rest 10 min before serving.

Tips — Always start the machine before pouring. If the paste is chunky, sieve the custard before chilling. The pistachio paste recipe makes more than needed — leftovers keep in the fridge for a week. Never store ice cream in the freezer bowl.
