Oven Roasted Chicken
with Red Pesto

Red pesto crust · Lemon · Quick roast

10 min
25 min
~40 min
4 servings

Ingredients

1kg Chicken breasts
80g Red pesto (sun-dried tomato pesto)
1 tbsp Olive oil
2 Garlic cloves, crushed
½ Lemon, juiced
½ tsp Salt
¼ tsp Black pepper

Method

  1. Pat the chicken breasts dry. Season both sides with salt and pepper.

  2. Mix red pesto, olive oil and crushed garlic in a small bowl. Spread thickly over the top of each breast.

  3. Arrange the breasts in a roasting tray and squeeze the lemon juice over them.

  4. Roast at 200°C (fan) for about 25 minutes, until the pesto is bubbling and the chicken is just cooked through (74°C internal at the thickest point). Thicker breasts or a fuller tray may need longer — go by temperature, not the clock.

  5. Cover loosely with foil and let the breasts rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Tips — No thermometer? Cut into the thickest part — juices should run clear with no pink. Great with cherry tomatoes scattered in the tray if you have them, roasted alongside the chicken. Leftovers keep refrigerated for up to 3 days.
