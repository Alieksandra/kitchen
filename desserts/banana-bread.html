Whole Wheat
Banana Bread

Yogurt · Maple syrup · Whole wheat

15 min
60–65 min
~1.5 hrs
1 loaf

Ingredients

288g Mashed ripe bananas (~3 medium)
90g Turkisk yogurt 10%
2 Large eggs
85g Pure maple syrup
50g Packed light brown sugar
80ml Whole milk
½ tsp Vanilla powder
220g Whole wheat flour
30g Vanilla whey protein powder
1 tsp Baking soda
½ tsp Ground cinnamon
½ tsp Fine salt
67g Chopped walnuts (optional)

Method

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower third position and preheat to 175°C. Grease a 23×13cm loaf pan.

  2. Beat mashed bananas, Turkisk yogurt and eggs together until combined. Add maple syrup, brown sugar, milk and vanilla powder. Mix until combined.

  3. Whisk together flour, protein powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Add to wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in walnuts if using.

  4. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 60–65 minutes, loosely covering with foil halfway through. Done when a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out with only a few moist crumbs.

  5. Cool completely in the pan on a wire rack before slicing. Tastes best on day 2!

Tips — Use very ripe bananas with brown spots for best sweetness and moisture. Greek yogurt replaces applesauce 1:1 and adds a slight tang. Keeps at room temperature for 2 days or refrigerated for up to 1 week. Freezes well for up to 3 months.
